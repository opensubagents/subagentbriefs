Every tool you have today was built for humans doing the work. Hamster is for teams where agents deliver. You set the direction — the reasoning, constraints, and customer context — and Hamster captures it in a brief your whole team can align on.

Agents without direction deliver the wrong thing fast. Briefs make sure they deliver the right thing.

You review what shipped. You don't babysit how it's built.
