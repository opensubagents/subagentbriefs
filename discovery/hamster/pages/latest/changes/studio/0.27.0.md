# Get set up in minutes with guided onboarding

A step-by-step wizard walks you through workspace setup, team invites, and uploading company context.

## Onboarding Wizard

New users walk through account setup, team invites, and role selection in a single flow. Add your company website and upload brand documents during setup. [See the getting started guide](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/getting-started).

## Chat File Uploads

Drag and drop files into chat threads and Hamster can immediately read them. Upload a PDF of your product spec and Hamster references it when generating briefs or blueprints.

## Better Understanding

Hamster now understands your intent even when you phrase requests differently. Ask to "create a brief from this file" or "turn this document into a brief" and you get the same result.

If a response isn't quite right, hit "Try Again." Hamster takes another pass without you having to start over.

## Improvements

* URLs shared in chat now display as rich preview cards
