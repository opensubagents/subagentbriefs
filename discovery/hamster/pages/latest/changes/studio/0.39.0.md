# Hamster asks follow-ups when your answer is unclear

Structured questions now appear throughout the entire product, with automatic clarification for ambiguous answers.

## Structured Questions Everywhere

Hamster now uses interactive questions throughout discovery, briefs, and blueprints. Each question appears as selectable options you can click through, or type a custom answer.

When your answer is ambiguous, Hamster reprompts with a more specific question rather than proceeding on a guess.

## Improvements

* Attaching a document to a conversation now gives Hamster access to its full content

## Bug Fixes

* Fixed the "Next" button becoming unresponsive after navigating back to a previous question
* Fixed duplicate questions appearing when resuming a previously started questionnaire
* Fixed losing your place when canceling a questionnaire mid-way
