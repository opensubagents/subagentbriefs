# Bring your Slack conversations into the mix

Connect your Slack workspace so Hamster can reference team discussions when generating briefs.

## Slack Connector

Connect your Slack workspace and Hamster can search your team's conversations when generating briefs or blueprints. If your team discussed a feature in Slack, Hamster references those discussions automatically. Connect Slack from [Settings > Connections](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/connections/slack).

## URL Context

Paste a URL in chat and Hamster reads the page and uses it as context. Share your company website and Hamster pulls in your product information when generating documentation.

## Improvements

* The diff viewer now clearly shows what will change before you accept or reject edits
* Hamster now recovers automatically when something goes wrong mid-workflow instead of blocking your progress
* @mentions are highlighted in messages
* Related chat messages are now grouped into a single block
* Tables and code blocks display correctly in messages
* Pasting formatted text from other apps preserves the formatting
