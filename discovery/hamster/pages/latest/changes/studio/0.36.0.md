# Research that runs itself

Hamster now researches topics independently and surfaces findings in your thread.

## Web Research

When you ask Hamster to research a topic, it works through sources independently and brings findings back into your thread. You can watch progress in real time as it works. [Learn more about guided research](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/plans/guided-research).

## Improvements

* Chat messages now display timestamps

## Bug Fixes

* Fixed questions not appearing when there was exactly one question to ask
* Fixed navigation when clicking back from a sub-thread during research
