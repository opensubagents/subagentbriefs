# Watch your plan come together in real time

Plans now generate in a dedicated thread where you can watch progress and drill into any task.

## Threaded Plan Generation

Plans now generate in a dedicated thread with a card in the main chat. Click any task to see its details inline, then back out without losing your place.

## Research to Brief

After research completes, Hamster automatically turns findings into a brief or blueprint. The research picks up on key people, companies, and concepts and uses them to shape the output. [Learn more about briefs](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs).

## Ask Questions During Research

Hamster can now pause guided research to ask clarifying questions before proceeding. Once you respond, it resumes from where it stopped with your answers in context. [Learn more about guided research](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/plans/guided-research).

## Bug Fixes

* Fixed chat failing to load when navigating between briefs, blueprints, and methods
* Fixed infinite loading on the Library tab
* Fixed an intermittent sign-in crash
* Fixed plan thread navigation not returning to the correct view
