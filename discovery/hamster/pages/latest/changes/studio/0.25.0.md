# Teach Hamster how your company works

Blueprints and Methods give Hamster structured knowledge about your company and team processes.

## Blueprints

Create blueprints for different aspects of your company: product specs, company handbook, team processes. Blueprints generated during onboarding are automatically tailored to your company type. [Learn more about blueprints](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/blueprints).

## Methods

Document your team's processes and best practices as Methods. Link Methods to the blueprints they relate to, and Hamster references them when generating content. [Learn more about skills and methods](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/skills-methods).

## Improvements

* Answers about a specific topic now pull from the most relevant sections of your documents
* Show or hide columns in your workspace to match your workflow
* @mentions in chat now trigger notifications
