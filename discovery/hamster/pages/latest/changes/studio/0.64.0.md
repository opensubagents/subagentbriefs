# Tell Hamster what you need, all at once

A single chat creates briefs, blueprints, and initiatives together, with cross-document edit suggestions. Plus brief alignment in Slack and Linear/Jira sync badges.

## Unified chat

Hamster chat now creates multiple documents and proposes edits in the same conversation. Ask for a brief, a blueprint, and an initiative in one message, and Hamster creates them all.

![A Hamster chat showing two briefs being created and linked to an initiative in one turn](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/releases/studio/0.64.0/chat.webp)

Ask for edits to an existing brief or blueprint and Hamster opens a suggestion thread on the document.

[Learn more about chat](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/conversations).

## Brief alignment in Slack

Briefs now auto-post to your primary Slack channel as a card.

Open the flexpane in Slack to mark the brief as **Ready** or **Not yet**, change its status, or post a note. Each update lands on the brief activity timeline.

Pick a primary channel and toggle auto-posting from your Slack connection settings.

Connect Slack from [Settings > Connections](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/connections/slack).

## Linear and Jira sync badges

Briefs synced to Linear or Jira now show a sync badge that opens the linked project or epic. Linear-synced initiatives get the same badge.

![A brief header showing Linear and Jira sync badges with the issue identifiers, alongside the brief tags](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/releases/studio/0.64.0/sync-badges.webp)

Status changes on briefs now sync to Linear and Jira too. Move a brief to In Progress in Hamster and the linked Linear project or Jira epic moves to its mapped status.

Connect [Linear](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/connections/linear) or [Jira](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/connections/jira) from Settings > Connections.

## Improvements

* Team member roles can now be changed inline from the members table
* Reviewers no longer see onboarding steps and team settings they can't act on
* Slack Agent settings now save automatically
* Slack connections are now named **Slack Search** and **Slack Agent**
* `.docx` files are now supported as attachments
