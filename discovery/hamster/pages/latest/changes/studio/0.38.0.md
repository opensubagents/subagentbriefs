# Refine your plan through conversation

Chat directly with Hamster to add, remove, or restructure tasks in your plan.

## Plan Builder Chat Mode

Plans can now be refined through conversation. Open a plan thread and ask Hamster to add, remove, or restructure tasks. Changes apply directly to the live plan. [Learn more about conversations](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/conversations).

## Image Context for Briefs

Images you upload to briefs and blueprints are now automatically described by Hamster and factored into generated plans and content. Hover over any image in the editor to see its description. [Learn more about document analysis](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/ai-features/document-analysis).

## Improvements

* Plans now reference real files from your connected repos

## Bug Fixes

* Fixed duplicate messages at the end of Hamster's responses
* Fixed dark mode text color and list indentation in chat
* Fixed edits occasionally producing inconsistent formatting
* Fixed brief title not showing in plan cards
