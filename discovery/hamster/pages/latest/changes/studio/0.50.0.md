# Get Hamster up to speed during setup, not after

Connect GitHub, Linear, and more during onboarding. Synced tasks now show assignees who aren't in your workspace yet.

## Connect Tools at Setup

Onboarding now includes a step to [connect your tools](https://tryhamster.com/product/studio/connections) before you create your first brief. Connect early, and Hamster uses your issues, pull requests, and documents from the start.

![The Connect your tools onboarding step showing four service tiles, with GitHub and Linear already connected](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/releases/studio/0.50.0/connect-tools.webp)

Already set up? You'll find the same connections under your team settings. [See all available connections](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/connections/overview).

## Linear Assignee Invites

Tasks synced from Linear now show the assignee's name even if they haven't joined your workspace yet. Admins get a notification and can invite them in one click. Once they accept, Hamster automatically assigns all their pending tasks.

![A task list showing Alice Chen with a placeholder avatar on a synced task, a notification that she hasn't joined Hamster yet, and the Invite Members dialog with her email pre-filled](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/releases/studio/0.50.0/linear-assignee-invite.webp)

Connect your Linear workspace from [Settings > Connections](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/connections/linear).

## Bug Fixes

* Fixed a GitHub connection issue that caused "No GitHub token available" errors when creating briefs
