# Your briefs, organized the way you actually ship

Initiatives group briefs under the product launches, redesigns, and goals driving them.

## Initiatives

A flat list of [briefs](https://tryhamster.com/product/studio/briefs) works when you're small. You can now create initiatives to group related briefs together and track them as one effort.

![The Initiatives list showing eight initiatives with Shipping, Refining, and Draft state badges alongside Active and Planned status columns](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/releases/studio/0.52.0/initiatives-list.webp)

Each initiative has its own document. Write the goals, set the timeline, and see every linked brief in one place.

![The Q3 Product Launch initiative open with Goals, Timeline, and Success Criteria sections, and three associated briefs listed below](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/releases/studio/0.52.0/initiative-detail.webp)

Link any brief to an initiative. Search, select, done.

![The Add Brief modal showing a searchable list of available briefs with status badges and a Link Brief button](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/releases/studio/0.52.0/initiative-add-brief.webp)

[Get started with Initiatives](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/initiatives).
