# Keep your briefs organized as your team scales

Custom tags let you label and filter across every view. Initiatives now sync with Linear.

## Brief Tags

Tags are shared across your workspace. Create them inline or pick from existing ones your team has already defined.

![A brief detail page with design and onboarding tags applied and the tag popover open while searching for marketing](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/releases/studio/0.55.0/brief-tags.webp)

Once your team starts tagging, filter the briefs list to find exactly what you need.

![The briefs list filtered by two tags with the tag dropdown open showing design and mobile selected](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/releases/studio/0.55.0/briefs-filter.webp)

[Learn more about brief tags](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs/brief-tags).

## Initiative Linear Sync

Changes to initiatives now automatically reflect in both Hamster and Linear. [Learn more about initiatives](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/initiatives) and [set up Linear sync](https://tryhamster.com/docs/integrations/linear).

## Improvements

* The getting started screen now shows suggestions based on your role

## Bug Fixes

* Fixed onboarding issues that could stall account setup for Google sign-up and waitlisted users
