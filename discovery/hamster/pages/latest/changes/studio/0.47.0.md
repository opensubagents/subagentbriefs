# See your plan take shape as Hamster builds it

The redesigned Plan tab shows each phase of generation, plus Figma integration for briefs and blueprints.

## Plan Tab Redesign

Plan generation used to be a black box. Now you can watch it happen: each phase is tracked, and tasks stream into the list as they're created.

![The progress card showing "Building your plan..." with three phases, the first two checked off and the third active with a spinner, and three tasks visible in the Todo list below](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/releases/studio/0.47.0/progress.webp)

Once generation completes, you have a full plan ready to work from. Parent tasks break into subtasks, each with an owner and clear dependencies.

![The completed plan with three expandable parent tasks showing nested subtasks, assignee avatars, and progress label](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/releases/studio/0.47.0/task-list.webp)

Open any task to see the full picture: what needs to happen, who owns it, and what it depends on.

![The task detail view showing the description, metadata, subtasks list, and activity feed](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/releases/studio/0.47.0/task-detail.webp)

## Brief Management from Chat

You can now manage briefs directly from chat. Mark a brief as ready, cast an alignment vote, or notify your team. Type "mark this brief as In Review and let the team know" and it's done. [Learn more about briefs](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs).

## Figma Integration

Agents can now reference your Figma design files when generating briefs and blueprints. If a brief mentions a specific screen or component, Hamster pulls in the relevant designs instead of working from text alone.

Connect Figma from your [team's connections page](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/connections/figma) or the Context tab.

## Improvements

* Hamster retains memory of your conversation across turns and references earlier requirements correctly
* Hamster distinguishes between proposing something and actually doing it
* Chat suggestions are now enabled by default
* `hamster team switch` and `hamster team list` let you manage teams from the terminal
* Search results now rank more accurately
* Invite up to 25 team members at once (previously 5)

## Bug Fixes

* Fixed Hamster failing to read files and images uploaded in earlier messages
* Fixed documents containing blockquotes, tables, or task lists drifting on each edit cycle
