Plans

## From brief to full delivery plan in seconds.

Click once. Hamster reads your brief, checks your codebase and team context, then generates a structured task breakdown with acceptance criteria for every subtask. Tasks appear as they're created — you can start reviewing before generation finishes.

[Explore Plans→](https://tryhamster.com/product/studio/plans)

## Direction has a home.

When Hamster writes a brief, builds a plan, or executes for you — it's not guessing. It has all three.

Brief

### What to build next.

Each brief captures a single increment of work — the reasoning, constraints, customer context, and team decisions that shape the implementation.

Blueprint

### Why you build this way.

Your vision, strategy, personas, and architectural decisions — documented once and applied to every brief, plan, and task Hamster generates.

Skill

### How Hamster works for you.

Your team's methods and workflows — applied automatically when Hamster generates briefs, plans, and executes tasks.

## Everything else you need.

### Thread Branches

Fork any conversation into a focused sub-thread. AI writes a summary and shares it back when you're done.

### Voice

Talk to the AI assistant hands-free with a keyboard shortcut. Think out loud, get structured output.

See who reviewed a brief, who has concerns, and how alignment is tracking across your team.

### Context Documents

Attach files and URLs to any brief. The AI reads them and uses them when generating plans and answering questions.

### Skills & Methods

Document how your team works. Skills and methods are applied automatically when Hamster generates briefs and plans.

### Document Versioning

Every change to every document is tracked automatically. View history and restore any earlier version.
