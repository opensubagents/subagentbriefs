# AI that knows your company.

Document your vision, strategy, personas, and ways of working once. Hamster draws on your blueprints every time it helps you plan or write — so the output always reflects your actual company context.

Structure

## A hierarchy that grows with you.

Start with a root blueprint for your company, then expand into child documents for specific areas — product strategy, engineering standards, personas, go-to-market context. The hierarchy is yours to define. Navigate it like a document tree.

![Blueprint hierarchy showing nested organizational documents](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/blueprints/creating-blueprints/blueprints-list-view-desktop-light.webp?v=6)![Blueprint hierarchy showing nested organizational documents](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/blueprints/creating-blueprints/blueprints-list-view-desktop-dark.webp?v=6)

What to document

## Write it once. Used everywhere.

Blueprints are referenced automatically when you generate a plan, write a brief, or ask the AI a question. The more context you document, the more accurate and relevant the AI's output becomes.

Company context

Mission, vision, strategy, and what you're building and why.

User personas

Who your customers are, their goals, frustrations, and how they make decisions.

Engineering standards

Coding conventions, architecture patterns, and technical constraints.

Ways of working

How your team runs discovery, planning, reviews, and delivery.

Product principles

The decisions and tradeoffs that define how you build your product.

Key capabilities

### Full document editor

Write and edit blueprints in a rich text editor with built-in AI assistance.

### Document hierarchy

Organize blueprints into a tree structure. Navigate with a collapsible sidebar.

### Real-time collaboration

Edit blueprints with your team simultaneously. Changes are visible to everyone instantly.

### Automatic context injection

Blueprints are automatically searched and referenced when the AI generates plans or answers questions.

### AI editing assistant

Ask the AI to improve, expand, or restructure blueprint content directly in the editor.

### Version history

Every change is tracked. Restore any earlier version of any blueprint document.

### Guided question flows

During blueprint flows, agents ask structured clarifying questions and reprompt when answers are too vague to act on — rather than proceeding on incomplete context.
