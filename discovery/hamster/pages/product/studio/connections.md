# Context from every tool, automatically.

Connect the tools your team already uses. The AI searches across every connected source when you write a brief or ask a question — so you never have to paste in background information manually.

Issue tracker sync

## Keep your issue tracker in sync.

Two-way sync between Hamster and your issue tracker. Tasks created in Hamster appear in Linear or Jira. Status changes flow in both directions. Tickets close as tasks complete — no one touches them.

Your team and leadership stay informed in the tools they already use. No workflow changes required.

[Learn about issue tracker sync→](https://tryhamster.com/product/studio/sync)

01

Hamster reads your tickets

Connect Linear or Jira. Hamster reads your existing issues and writes briefs that fill in the reasoning your backlog never captured.

02

Tasks sync both ways

Tasks created in Hamster appear in your tracker. Status, priority, and assignee changes flow in both directions automatically.

03

Tickets close themselves

As Hamster tasks complete, the corresponding tickets update. Leadership gets visibility without changing tools.

How connections work

## Set up once. Every team member benefits.

Connections are configured once at the team level. The AI automatically searches across all connected sources whenever it helps you write, plan, or answer a question. No individual setup, no copy-pasting context.

01

Connect your tools

Go to Settings → Connections. Authenticate with each service once.

02

AI searches automatically

When you write a brief or generate a plan, Hamster searches all connected sources for relevant context.

03

Output reflects your reality

Plans reference existing issues. Briefs reference existing docs. Answers reflect actual team decisions.
