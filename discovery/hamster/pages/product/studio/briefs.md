# The AI-native product document.

A brief is the central document for any project — capturing goals, requirements, and context in one place. Write it once, and every plan, task, and agent run derives from it.

![Brief editor with collaborative cursors, AI thread summary, and rich editing tools](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/briefs/editing-briefs/brief-editor-summary-desktop-light.webp?v=6)![Brief editor with collaborative cursors, AI thread summary, and rich editing tools](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/briefs/editing-briefs/brief-editor-summary-desktop-dark.webp?v=6)

Writing

## A full editor, with AI built in.

Write in a rich document editor with real-time AI assistance in the side panel. Ask questions, request rewrites, or let the AI expand a rough idea into a full brief section. Collaborative cursors show your teammates working alongside you. A version history timeline tracks every saved state — compare any two versions with a structural diff and restore with one click.

![Brief document view showing the rich text editor](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/briefs/editing-briefs/brief-editor-populated-desktop-light.webp?v=6)![Brief document view showing the rich text editor](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/briefs/editing-briefs/brief-editor-populated-desktop-dark.webp?v=6)

Context

## Attach everything the AI needs.

Drop in files, paste URLs, or link documents from Google Drive or Notion. Hamster fetches and indexes every attachment server-side — so when you ask a question or generate a plan, the AI is working from the full text of your actual research, not a blank slate.

![Context documents view showing attached files and URLs](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/briefs/context-documents/context-view-desktop-light.webp?v=6)![Context documents view showing attached files and URLs](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/briefs/context-documents/context-view-desktop-dark.webp?v=6)

Alignment

## Know when the team is aligned.

The activity timeline shows who has reviewed the brief, who has raised concerns, and how readiness is tracking across the team. Alignment happens in the brief — not in a separate meeting or Slack thread.

![Activity timeline showing team alignment status](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/briefs/activity-timeline/activity-view-desktop-light.webp?v=6)![Activity timeline showing team alignment status](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/briefs/activity-timeline/activity-view-desktop-dark.webp?v=6)

Key capabilities

* Real-time collaborative editing with live cursors
* AI assistant in the editor side panel
* Brief status lifecycle (Draft → In Review → Ready)
* File and URL attachments indexed for AI context
* Team alignment votes and readiness tracking
* Version history timeline — browse every saved version, compare any two with a structural diff, and restore with one click
* @mention teammates anywhere in the document
* Share briefs with specific team members
* Structured question flows — agents ask clarifying questions throughout discovery, brief, and blueprint flows; if your answer is too vague, the agent reprompts rather than guessing
