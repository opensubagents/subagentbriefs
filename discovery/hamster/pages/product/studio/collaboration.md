Conversations

## Work happens in threads.

Every session starts as a conversation. Chat with the AI, share context with teammates, and take action — all in a persistent thread that lives alongside your briefs. Nothing gets lost.

![New conversation view showing the chat interface](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/conversations/starting-conversation/new-conversation-desktop-light.webp?v=6)![New conversation view showing the chat interface](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/conversations/starting-conversation/new-conversation-desktop-dark.webp?v=6)

Thread Branches

## Focus mode for any conversation.

Fork any message into a focused sub-thread to dig into a detail without cluttering the main chat. The branch has its own AI context. When you're done, the AI writes a summary and shares it back to the main thread.

![Sub-thread branched from a message showing focused discussion with AI summary](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/conversations/thread-branches/thread-branch-view-desktop-light.webp?v=6)![Sub-thread branched from a message showing focused discussion with AI summary](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/conversations/thread-branches/thread-branch-view-desktop-dark.webp?v=6)

Team

## Invite your whole team.

Add teammates to your workspace. They see the same briefs, plans, and conversations. Assign roles to control what each person can do. @mention anyone in a document or thread to bring their attention to specific content.

![Team members list showing workspace members and their roles](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/teams/inviting-members/members-list-desktop-light.webp?v=6)![Team members list showing workspace members and their roles](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/teams/inviting-members/members-list-desktop-dark.webp?v=6)

Key capabilities

### Real-time editing

Multiple teammates edit the same document simultaneously. Changes and cursors visible to everyone instantly.

### Thread branches

Fork any message into a sub-thread. AI summarizes and shares the conclusion back to the main thread.

### @mentions

Mention teammates anywhere in a document or conversation to bring their attention to specific content.

### Document versioning

Every change to every brief, skill, or method is tracked. View history and restore any earlier version.

### Activity timeline

See who reviewed a brief, who has concerns, and how alignment is tracking across the team.

### Roles and permissions

Control what each person can do by assigning them the appropriate role in your workspace.
