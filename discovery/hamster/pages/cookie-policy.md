# Cookie Policy

## Last updated February 2026

## 1\. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and improving our services.

## 2\. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

* **Essential Cookies:** Required for basic website functionality
* **Performance Cookies:** Help us understand how visitors use our site
* **Functional Cookies:** Remember your preferences and settings
* **Analytics Cookies:** Provide insights into website usage patterns

## 3\. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

* **Session Cookies:** Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
* **Persistent Cookies:** Remain on your device for a set period
* **First-Party Cookies:** Set directly by our website
* **Third-Party Cookies:** Set by external services we use

## 4\. Specific Cookies

Here are some specific cookies we use and their purposes:

| Cookie Name | Purpose                               | Duration |
| ----------- | ------------------------------------- | -------- |
| session\_id | Maintains your login session          | Session  |
| preferences | Stores your site preferences          | 1 year   |
| analytics   | Tracks website usage for improvements | 2 years  |

## 5\. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

* **Analytics Services:** Help us understand how our website is used
* **Payment Processors:** Required for processing payments
* **Social Media:** Enable social media integration features

## 6\. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### 4.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. You can:

* Delete existing cookies
* Block cookies from being set
* Set your browser to ask for permission before setting cookies
* Set your browser to automatically delete cookies when you close it

Note: Disabling certain cookies may limit your ability to use some features of our website.

### 4.2 Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie banner that allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by:

* Clicking the cookie settings link in our footer
* Clearing your browser cookies and refreshing the page
* Contacting us directly

### 4.3 Third-Party Opt-Outs

For third-party cookies, you can opt out through the respective service providers:

* [Google Analytics Opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
* [Network Advertising Initiative](https://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/)
* [Your Online Choices](https://www.youronlinechoices.com/)

## 7\. What Information Do Cookies Collect?

Cookies may collect various types of information, including:

* **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
* **Usage Information:** Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked, search queries
* **Preference Information:** Language settings, theme preferences, account settings
* **Authentication Information:** Login status, session tokens, user permissions
* **Performance Information:** Page load times, error rates, user interactions

This information is typically collected anonymously and used to improve our services and user experience.

## 8\. How We Use Cookie Data

We use the information collected through cookies for various purposes:

* **Website Functionality:** Ensuring the website works properly and securely
* **User Experience:** Personalizing content and remembering your preferences
* **Analytics:** Understanding how our website is used and identifying areas for improvement
* **Security:** Protecting against fraud and unauthorized access
* **Performance:** Monitoring website performance and optimizing loading times
* **Marketing:** Delivering relevant advertisements and measuring campaign effectiveness

## 9\. How Long Do We Keep Cookie Data?

The duration for which we store cookie data depends on the type of cookie:

* **Session Cookies:** Deleted when you close your browser
* **Persistent Cookies:** Remain on your device for a set period (usually 1-2 years)
* **Essential Cookies:** Kept for as long as necessary for security and functionality
* **Analytics Cookies:** Typically retained for 26 months (Google Analytics standard)

You can delete cookies at any time through your browser settings, which will remove the stored data immediately.

## 10\. International Data Transfers

Some of our third-party cookie providers may transfer data to countries outside your jurisdiction. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and implement appropriate safeguards.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we rely on adequacy decisions, standard contractual clauses, and other appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

## 11\. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of any material changes by:

* Posting the updated policy on our website
* Updating the "Last updated" date
* Sending email notifications for significant changes
* Displaying a prominent notice on our website

We encourage you to review this Cookie Policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies and protect your information.
