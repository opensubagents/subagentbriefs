# AI-Native SDLC

The traditional SDLC was built for a world without AI. It's time for a new lifecycle — one where AI assists every step.

Requirements → Design → Development → Testing → Deployment. The classic waterfall or agile variants worked for decades. But they assume humans are doing all the work.

**The AI-native SDLC is different:**

* **Brief** — Write what you want with AI assistance
* **Align** — Get stakeholder consensus in minutes
* **Plan** — AI generates structured tasks from briefs
* **Execute** — AI agents help implement the plan
* **Ship** — Measure outcomes against goals

Hamster and Taskmaster together implement this new lifecycle. Planning in Hamster, execution with Taskmaster.
