# The Velocity Gap

Your devs ship in hours now. You still operate in weeks. The bottleneck moved — and it's the planning layer.

AI coding tools like Cursor, Claude Code, and Copilot have fundamentally changed how fast developers can ship code. But product requirements still take days to write. Alignment meetings still eat calendars. The spec is outdated before engineering reads it.

**This is the velocity gap.** Engineering can move at AI speed. Everything upstream of engineering cannot.

Hamster closes this gap by giving product teams AI-native tools that match engineering's velocity. Write briefs faster, align in minutes, and generate plans that are ready for execution.
