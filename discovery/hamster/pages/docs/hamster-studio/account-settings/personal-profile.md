# Personal Profile

Update the name, avatar, and email address that represent you across Hamster Studio.

## Overview

Your personal profile is separate from any team or workspace you belong to. Changes you make here — your display name, profile picture, and email address — apply to your account everywhere: in comments, assignee fields, member lists, and notifications. You can update these at any time without affecting your team settings.

![Personal settings page showing avatar upload, name, and email fields](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/account-settings/personal-profile/personal-settings-desktop-light.webp?v=6)![Personal settings page showing avatar upload, name, and email fields](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/account-settings/personal-profile/personal-settings-desktop-dark.webp?v=6) 

## How It Works

1. Open personal settings — Select your avatar or name in the sidebar to open the settings menu, then navigate to Personal Profile. The URL pattern is `/home/[your-team]/personal-settings/profile`.
2. Update your avatar — In the Account Image section, upload a new profile picture. Supported formats include standard image types. The image appears as a circular avatar wherever your name is shown.
3. Change your display name — In the Name section, type a new display name (2–100 characters) and save. This is the name other team members see when you're mentioned or assigned to tasks.
4. Update your email address — In the Email section, enter a new email address. Hamster sends a confirmation link to the new address. Your email change takes effect after you click that link.

## Key Capabilities

* **Avatar upload**: Upload a profile image directly from your device. It replaces your previous avatar immediately after saving.
* **Display name**: A free-text name shown throughout the product — in [Brief](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs) assignee fields, member lists, comments, and activity feeds.
* **Email address**: Your sign-in email. Changing it requires email confirmation for security. If you signed in with a social provider (Google, GitHub, or Microsoft), your email is managed by that provider.

## Tips

* Use a recognizable name — ideally your first name or the name your teammates know you by. This makes it easier for others to find you in assignee pickers and mentions.
* If you need to change the email address associated with a social sign-in (Google, GitHub, or Microsoft), update it with that provider first. See [Security](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/account-settings/security) for how linked accounts work.
* Your avatar is displayed at small sizes throughout the product. A high-contrast headshot or simple icon works better than a wide landscape image.
