# Preferences

Adjust how Hamster Studio looks and behaves for you personally.

## Overview

Preferences let you customize the Hamster Studio interface to suit how you work. Settings you change here apply only to your account — they don't affect what other team members see. Currently, preferences include your choice of interface theme. Additional preference options may appear here as the product evolves.

![Preferences settings page showing the interface theme selector with light and dark options](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/account-settings/preferences/preferences-desktop-light.webp?v=6)![Preferences settings page showing the interface theme selector with light and dark options](https://tryhamster.com/_site/images/features/account-settings/preferences/preferences-desktop-dark.webp?v=6) 

## How It Works

1. Open preferences — Navigate to the settings menu and select Preferences.
2. Choose a theme — The Theme section presents three options: Light, Dark, and System. Click the button for the theme you want to use. The change applies immediately — there is no save button.

## Theme Options

| Option | What it does                                                                                                            |
| ------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Light  | Uses a light background with dark text throughout the interface.                                                        |
| Dark   | Uses a dark background with light text throughout the interface.                                                        |
| System | Follows your operating system's current appearance setting, switching automatically when you change it at the OS level. |

## Tips

* "System" is the recommended option for most users — it matches your OS preference and switches automatically between day and night without any manual action.
* If you work in a shared environment (e.g., presenting from your screen), switching to Light mode temporarily makes the interface easier to read on a projector or in a bright room.
* Your theme choice is stored with your account. If you sign in from a different browser or device, Hamster will apply your saved preference there too once the page loads.
