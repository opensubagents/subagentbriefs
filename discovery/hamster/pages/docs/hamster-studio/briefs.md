# Briefs

The single artifact that carries an idea from "we should do this" to a pull request.

## Overview

A [Brief](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs) is the spine of Hamster Studio. One [Brief](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs) is one team-aligned unit of "what we're going to ship" — title, description, [Plan](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/plans) of parent [Tasks](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/tasks) and subtasks, tags, status, alignment votes, and a deliver button that hands the work to either [Cloud Agents](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/cloud-agents) or your own [CLI](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/cli). Everything else in the product — [Initiatives](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/initiatives), [Hamster Chat](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/chat), [Plan](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/plans) generation, [Delivery](https://tryhamster.com/docs/concepts/delivery), sync — exists in service of getting [Briefs](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs) to a state where the [Team](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/teams) will commit to them and an AI agent can deliver on them.

[Briefs](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs) are where the Refine → Align → Deliver loop plays out. You **refine** a [Brief](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs) in chat until the scope is sharp. You **align** the team by sharing it and collecting alignment votes. You **deliver** by pressing one button and getting a PR back, without leaving the brief.

Most [Briefs](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs) start in chat: you describe what you want, the AI agent comes back with a draft [Brief](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs) in its own thread, and you take it from there. [Briefs](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs) are also the spec layer the AI consumes — the clearer the [Brief](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs), the better the [Plan](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/plans) and the better the delivery.

You can deliver a [Brief](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs) to GitHub directly from a conversation — just tell Hamster what to ship, and it dispatches the job inline, without leaving the chat.

* [Brief Tags](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs/brief-tags) — Labelling and filtering [Briefs](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs) by topic, team, or theme

* [Activity Timeline](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs/activity-timeline) — Version-grouped alignment votes, status changes, and [Brief](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs) history
* [Sharing Briefs](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs/sharing-briefs) — Pulling teammates into a [Brief](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs) thread
