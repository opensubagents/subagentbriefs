# Plans

A structured, AI-generated [Task](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/tasks) breakdown that transforms your [Brief](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs) into actionable work your team can track and deliver.

## Overview

[Plans](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/plans) are the output of asking Hamster Studio's AI to turn a [Brief](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs) into deliverable work. Once a [Plan](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/plans) is generated, it appears as an ordered list of parent [Tasks](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/tasks) — each broken down into detailed subtasks with descriptions and acceptance criteria. [Plans](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/plans) live inside the [Brief](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs) they were generated from, and you can view, reorder, assign, and track every [Task](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/tasks) without leaving the brief.
