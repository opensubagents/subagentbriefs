# Account Settings

Manage your personal profile, security, preferences, and billing from one place.

## Overview

[Account Settings](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/account-settings) are split into two categories: personal settings that apply to you as an individual regardless of which team you're on, and team-level settings that apply to the workspace you're currently working in.

**Personal settings** — accessible from any team workspace under your name or avatar — include:

* [Personal Profile](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/account-settings/personal-profile) — Update your display name, avatar, and email address.
* [Security](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/account-settings/security) — Change your password, set up multi-factor authentication, and manage linked sign-in methods.
* [Preferences](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/account-settings/preferences) — Choose your interface theme (light, dark, or system).

**Team-level settings** — visible only to members with the right permissions — include:

* [Billing](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/account-settings/billing) — View your team's subscription, select or change a [Plan](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/plans), and access the billing portal.
