# Blueprints

Your workspace's single source of truth — the organizational context that shapes everything Hamster Studio does.

## Overview

[Blueprints](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/blueprints) are where you capture who your company is: your vision, strategy, personas, ways of working, and anything else that provides context for your work. They are structured, editable documents that live in a hierarchy you define. Everything you write here becomes available to the AI when it helps you create [Briefs](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs), generate [Plans](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/plans), and answer questions — so your work always reflects your actual company context.
