# Teams

Collaborate with colleagues inside a shared workspace where everyone works on the same projects, [Briefs](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs), and context.

## Overview

Team workspaces in Hamster Studio are shared environments where multiple people can create [Briefs](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs), run conversations, manage projects, and build on each other's work. Each team has its own workspace, separate from your personal account.

This section covers everything you need to manage a team:

* [Creating a Team](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/teams/creating-teams) — Create a new team workspace and get it ready for your colleagues.
* [Inviting Members](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/teams/inviting-members) — Send invitations, track pending invites, and accept requests to join a team.
* [Roles & Permissions](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/teams/roles-permissions) — Understand what owners and members can do, and how to change someone's role.
* [Team Profile](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/teams/team-profile) — Update your team's name and logo, and manage workspace-level settings.

## Where to Go Next

Once your team is set up, connect your tools in [Connections Overview](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/connections/overview) or start working together on [Briefs](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs/creating-briefs).
