# Connections

Connect your team's tools so Hamster has the context it needs to help you work.

## Overview

[Connections](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/connections) let you link the services your team already uses — GitHub, Linear, Figma, Slack, Notion, and others — directly to your Hamster workspace. Once connected, those sources become part of the context Hamster draws on when you chat, write [Briefs](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs), or generate plans.

You set up [Connections](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/connections) once at the team level. Every team member benefits automatically. Hamster searches across all connected sources when answering questions, so you get more accurate and relevant output without pasting in background information manually.

[Connections](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/connections) are managed from **Settings > [Connections](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/connections)** in your workspace.

Available now: GitHub, Linear, Figma, Slack, Notion, Cursor, Jira (requires enablement), Google Drive (requires enablement).
