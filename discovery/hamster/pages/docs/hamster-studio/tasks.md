# Tasks

The actionable work items generated from your [Plan](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/plans) — each one scoped, assigned, and tracked through to completion.

## Overview

[Tasks](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/tasks) are what your [Plan](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/plans) becomes when it is ready to execute. After a [Brief](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs) is reviewed and a [Plan](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/plans) is generated, each item in that [Plan](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/plans) becomes a [Task](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/tasks) your team can own. [Tasks](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/tasks) carry a title, description, status, priority, and assignee. They can be broken down further into subtasks, reordered, and moved between status columns as work progresses.
