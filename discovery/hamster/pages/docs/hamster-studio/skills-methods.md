# Skills & Methods

Document your team's processes, standards, and practices in one place — and put them to work across every [Brief](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs) and plan.

## Overview

Skills and [Methods](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/skills-methods) are two complementary ways to capture how your team works. Together they form a living [Knowledge](https://tryhamster.com/docs/concepts/knowledge) base that Hamster Studio draws on when it helps you write [Briefs](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/briefs), generate [Plans](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/plans), and guide delivery.

**Skills** are individual capabilities or [Task](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/tasks) types — a focused ability like "User Interview" or "Code Review" that describes something your team knows how to do. **[Methods](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/skills-methods)** are broader processes or frameworks — structured approaches like "Agile Workflow" or "Go to Market [Plan](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/plans)" that span multiple steps or documents. A skill can be associated with a [Method](https://tryhamster.com/docs/hamster-studio/skills-methods), but it does not have to be.

Both are organized by category, can be activated or deactivated at any time, and can be added from a curated library of templates or created from scratch.

_This feature may need to be enabled for your workspace._
