## Why

One paragraph. The reasoning, the constraints, the customer context.
Why does this brief exist? What ruled-out approaches led here?

## Outcome

What does done look like? A scenario, not a feature list.

## Acceptance

1. First criterion as a sentence
2. Second criterion as a sentence
3. Each one independently checkable

## Non-goals

- What this is not
- What we explicitly will not do
- Each as a bullet

## Working backwards

```
END        the outcome above
                    ↑
last step  what immediately precedes the outcome
                    ↑
prior step what unblocks the last step
                    ↑
START      what unblocks the prior step (i.e. now)
```
